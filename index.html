ABOUT OUR - Correspondent

Welcome To Sri Vani Degree & PG College

"Dr. N. Narayanamma" is the Founder Correspondent of Sri Vani Degree and PG College. She is not only a well known Physician in the town of Anatapuramu, she is also known for her social service such as running Orphanages, free hostel for Harijan girls, a well known school in the town. She worked pro bono in Government Hospital for decades rendering free services. With this background and recognizing the dire need for quality education, she started with a Junior College for girls with a strength of 14 girls. These humble beginnings with her dedication and hard work have blossomed into the Degree, PG, MBA/MCA colleges.

As someone who is close to the Society and strives to contribute towards the betterment, she also has donated Rs. 2,00,000 towards Chief Ministers Relief fund to fight COVID. She also routinely awards gold medals to students who achieve University ranks.

She also loved farming and brought awareness about new techniques in cultivation in her village Illuru. Her hard work, sincerity & dedication she has taken the college to the level at which it is now. She is an inspiration to all the staff and students of Sri Vani Educational Society.
